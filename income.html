Income

The amount earnad by the person directly or indirectly by account of work,is known as Income

Example: Salary,Rent,bonus,etc

There are two types of incomes:
1. Direct Income
2. Indirect Income

Direct Income:

The Direct income is one which is earned directly by way of business activities.
Example: Salaried, Professionals. Indirect income is one which is earned by way of non-business activities.

Indirect Income:

Indirect income refers to earnings a business or individual receives from sources not directly tied to their primary, day-to-day operations. It is considered secondary income, often generated through investments, assets, or non-core business activities, and is recorded in the profit and loss account rather than trading accounts.
Examples: Earnings from savings accounts, fixed deposits, or loans, rent recieve, salary recieve, insurance recieve ,etc.
There are four primary types of incomes:
  1. Passive Income
  2. Active Income
  3. Portfolio Income
  4. Government Income
